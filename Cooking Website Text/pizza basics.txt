    <h2>Click on an instruction below to see quantities.</h2>    
    <p>Making a Pepperoni Pizza.</p>
    <p id="pizza1Text">To make the pizza base.<br>Mix the salt and yeast together in the warm water. Place flour in a bowl, make a well in the centre and add the water mixture along with the oil. </p>    
    <p id="pizza2Text">Use a wooden spoon to mix everything until you have a wet ball of dough. Put on floured surface and knead for 5mins.</p> 
    <p id="pizza3Text">Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with tea towel. Leave at room temperature to prove for 30mins.  The dough should double in size.</p>     
    <p id="pizza4Text">Shape the dough into a round pizza base and stab with a fork several times.  Spread with pizza topping tomato puree.</p>   
    <p id="pizza5Text">Add chopped tomatoes on top then add onion and garlic.</p>
    <p id="pizza6Text">Sprinkle over cheese and arrange Pepperoni.</p>
    <p id="pizza7Text">Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle on basil (1tsp dried basil may be used instead).</p>
    <p id="pizza8Text">Place the pizza on oiled baking sheets. Cook at 200 degrees for 20mins.</p>
