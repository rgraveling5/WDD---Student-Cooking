Making a Pepperoni Pizza.


To make the pizza base.
Mix the salt and yeast together in the warm water. Place flour in a bowl, make a well in the centre and add the water mixture along with the oil.


Use a wooden spoon to mix everything until you have a wet ball of dough. Put on floured surface and knead for 5mins.


Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with tea towel. Leave at room temperature to prove for 30mins. The dough should double in size.


Shape the dough into a round pizza base and stab with a fork several times. Spread with pizza topping tomato puree.


Add chopped tomatoes on top then add onion and garlic.


Sprinkle over cheese and arrange Pepperoni.


Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle on basil (1tsp dried basil may be used instead).


Place the pizza on oiled baking sheets. Cook at 200 degrees for 20mins.
